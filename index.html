👋 Welcome to BagTag
Please take a moment to read our terms

BagTag is a smart inventory organiser designed to help schools and businesses keep track of their belongings and supplies — anytime, anywhere.

✅ What BagTag is For
  • Organising and tracking everyday inventory and belongings
  • Personal use at home or professional use at your workplace
  • Simple day to day inventory management for schools and businesses
❌ What BagTag is NOT For
  • Do not store sensitive financial information such as banking details or passwords
  • Do not store personal information about others without their knowledge
  • BagTag is not an accounting, financial or staff management tool
⏱️ Free Trial & Subscription
  • Every new account enjoys a free trial with up to 10 bags — no payment required
  • Trial accounts can add unlimited items within those 10 bags
  • A monthly subscription unlocks unlimited bags and all Pro features
  • If a subscription is not activated, your account will be restricted but your data will be safely retained for 30 days
🔒 Your Data
  • Your data is stored on secure encrypted cloud servers
  • Your information is private and is never sold or shared with anyone
  • You can request deletion of your data anytime by writing to us at bagtag2026@gmail.com
⚠️ Your Responsibility
  • BagTag and its operators are not liable for any loss or misuse of information stored in the app
  • If you feel your account has been compromised please sign out immediately and contact us at bagtag2026@gmail.com
🇮🇳 Governing Law
  • BagTag is governed by the laws of India
  • Any disputes will be subject to the jurisdiction of Indian courts
