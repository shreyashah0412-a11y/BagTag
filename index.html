👋 Welcome to BagTag
Please take a moment to read our terms

BagTag is a simple and smart inventory organiser designed to help individuals and businesses keep track of their belongings and supplies — anytime, anywhere.

✅ What BagTag is For
  • Organising and tracking everyday inventory and belongings
  • Personal use at home or professional use at your workplace
  • Simple day to day inventory management
❌ What BagTag is NOT For
  • Do not store sensitive financial information such as banking details, passwords or financial documents
  • Do not store personal information about others without their knowledge
  • Do not store details of high value assets — BagTag is an organiser, not a secure vault
  • BagTag is not an accounting, financial or staff management tool
⏱️ Free Trial & Subscription
  • Every new account enjoys a 15 day free trial with full access — no payment required
  • After 15 days a monthly subscription is required to continue
  • You will be notified before your trial ends
  • If a subscription is not activated your account will be restricted but your data will be safely retained for 30 days
🔒 Your Data
  • Your data is stored on secure encrypted cloud servers
  • Your information is private and is never sold or shared with anyone
  • You can request deletion of your data anytime by writing to us at hello@bagtag.in
⚠️ Your Responsibility

  • BagTag and its operators are not liable for any loss or misuse of information stored in the app
  • If you feel your account has been compromised please sign out immediately and contact us at hello@bagtag.in
